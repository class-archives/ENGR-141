Question No 1:
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
What are the dimensions of the bins utilized by the separator/shredder?


The bin are rectangular prisms 5cm width/depth, and 7cm height.  At the top of the bin is a rigged rectangular handle across the entire width forming a 2 cm gap between the top of the bin and the handle. The handle is centered in the depth of the bin. The handle is 3 mm thick.  At the bottom of the bin is a forklift-style slots (e.g. shipping palette) with its open slots parallel to the gap of the handle. There are two symmetric forklift slots 1 cm tall and at least 1.5 cm wide. On the two sides that support the handle are a narrow textured strip (scallop ridges) for picking up/gripping the bin.   These strips are centered on the side of and runs vertically for 4 cm along the side of the bin and are ~3mm in width.
